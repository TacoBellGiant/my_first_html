🌞 Our Solar System

Explore the planets orbiting our Sun

☀️ Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of our solar system. It provides light and heat for all planets.

☿ Mercury

The smallest planet and the closest to the Sun. It has very hot days and very cold nights.

♀ Venus

Venus is the hottest planet. It has thick clouds that trap heat.

🌍 Earth

The only known planet with life. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered with water.

♂ Mars

Known as the Red Planet because of its reddish surface caused by iron oxide.

♃ Jupiter

The largest planet in the solar system. It has a giant storm called the Great Red Spot.

♄ Saturn

Famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.

♅ Uranus

An ice giant that rotates on its side.

♆ Neptune

The farthest planet from the Sun and known for very strong winds.
